Facilitator Development Program

A program designed to transform facilitators into digital mentors, equipping them with the skills, confidence, and tools to guide youth and MSMEs in co-building tech-driven solutions for social and economic growth.

    Facilitators are onboarded through immersive orientation sessions that introduce them to digital pedagogy, community accountability, and sponsor-ready reporting. They step into their role with excitement, knowing they are catalysts for transformation in their hubs and communities.
